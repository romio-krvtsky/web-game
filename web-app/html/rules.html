Rules

  1. The player's goal is to explain as many words as possible to each other before the time runs out.
  2. Players are not allowed to use parts and forms of the game word or use foreign translations.
  3. Every correctly guessed word scores the team a point, and each skipped word might incur a penalty depending on the game settings.
  4. The first team to reach a pre-set victory score will be crowned the winner!
